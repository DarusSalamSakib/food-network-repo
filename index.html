About Me

Hello guys, It’s me Sakib and trust me I am one of the biggest food freaks you’ll find around. I like all sort of cuisines especially Bengali and Indian ones. Kacchi Biryani, Beef Khichuri(hotchpotch), Chicken Biryani and other Mughal cuisines are my favourites. I use more Clarified Butter(Ghee) in case I cook any of these foods. It enriches the flavour and makes it more mouth-wateringly delicious..
